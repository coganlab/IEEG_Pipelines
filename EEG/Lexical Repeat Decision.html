Cogan Lab

 

PC: Stimulus Delivery PC (Windows 7)

 

Program: Psychtoolbox

 

Location: C:/Psychtoolbox_Scripts/Lexical_Repeat

 

Filename: Lexical_Repeat_WithinBlockEEG

 

Start subject: E2

 

Equipment Used: EEG system, Etymonic headphones, Cedrus response box, microphone

 

Command to run: Lexical_Repeat_WithinBlockEEG (subject, practice, startblock)

     subject = 'subject ID' (E#)

     practice = 0 (no) or 1(yes) 12 trials

     startblock = 1 for beginning, or n if interrupted and resuming task

 

Duration: 4 blocks total (32 trials per block); ~45 minutes total

 

Instructions:

Repeat: You will hear a series of words/non-words (funny sounding words that don't mean anything) and you are to repeat them.  The first thing you will see on the screen is a cue that says 'Listen'.  After that, you will hear either a word or a non-word.  After a short delay, the screen will say 'Speak' and you are to repeat the word/non-word you heard.  There are 2 blocks and they each take about 8 minutes.  You can stop at any time, and please use the breaks between blocks if you want to.

 

Decision: You will hear a series of words/non-words (funny sounding words that don't mean anything) and you are to say 'yes' if it is a word and 'No' if it is not.  The first thing you will see on the screen is a cue that says 'Listen'.  After that, you will hear either a word or a non-word.  After a short delay, the screen will say 'Speak' and you are to say if it is a word or not ('Yes' or 'No').  There are 2 blocks and they each take about 8 minutes.  You can stop at any time, and please use the breaks between blocks if you want to.

 

You will see a cue on the screen to either Repeat or Yes/No. You will then hear a word or non-word. When the screen shows 'Speak', repeat the word if the cue was 'Repeat' or decide if the word was a real word or non-word ny saying Yes or No if the cue was 'Yes/No'.
