Parking:

You can park in Duke Hospital Parking Garage II (PGII). The main entrance is on Elba St. There is a second entrance on Erwin Rd. You can cross Erwin Rd. to get to the Children's Hospital. There is also an underground walkway and shuttle service to the hospital. Both are located on the "RT" level of the parking garage. At the end of your visit you will receive a complimentary parking voucher, which can be validated at the designated kiosks before exiting the garage.

 

 

Collecting MRI Images

Once all of the participants arrive to the fish tank, walk towards the North Cafeteria, and a hallway on the left will take you to the BIAC scanner suite. 

  1. Have each participant complete the 90492_MR_Screening_Form.pdf
  2. Check to make sure any metal jewelry, belts, and items in pockets are removed before entering the scanner.  
  3. For female participants: give her the lunch box with an empty plastic cup. The bathroom is in the main hallway outside of the scanner suite. The most efficient method is to have males enter the scanner first, and then have female do the UA while the first scan is ongoing.  
  4. Participants may request copies of his/her scan, so have blank CDs in the black clipboard.
