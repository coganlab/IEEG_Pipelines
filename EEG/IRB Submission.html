For studies that involve human subjects, the department of Neurosurgery requires CRU review prior to submitting forms to the IRB. 

 

Research assistants should be CITI certified and will be included in the eIRB research personnel.

 

Complete the NSCRU Research Intake Form and include the following documentation: 

  1. Protocol/Research Summary
  2. Investigator's Brochure
  3. Lab Manual
  4. Draft ICF
  5. Recruitment Materials
  6. Grant Proposal
  7. Other attachments 

 

After the CRU review of the documents, approved items can be submitted to the IRB.
