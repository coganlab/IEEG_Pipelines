This is a general script to communicate to subjects and interested persons for the EEG study.

 

Email Inquiry

Dear [RECIPIENT NAME],

This is [RESEARCH STAFF NAME] from the Cogan lab, and I am contacting you to give you more information on the human cognition study being conducted by Dr. Greg Cogan. The purpose of this study is to better understand sensory, motor, and cognitive phenomena such as perception, memory, and language. This study involves an auditory, visual, and motor tasks on a computer while measuring electroencephalography (EEG) data, and creating an MRI as part of the second visit. This study is divided into two visits: Visit 1 will last approximately 2 hours and Visit 2 will not exceed 1 hour. The total duration of this study is 3 hours. 

I would like to schedule a phone call to ask you some questions that would help us determine if you qualify to be in this research study.  This will take about 15 minutes.  These questions will involve your giving information about your medical history.  Answering these questions is voluntary.  You are under no obligation to answer them, and not answering them will have no effect on your health care at Duke.  Not answering the questions, however, means that you will not be eligible to participate in this research study.  If at any time during this prescreening you would like to stop and not participate or if you have any questions, just let me know.

Please let me know a date, time, and phone number to contact you regarding qualifications for this study.

 

 

We look forward to hearing from you,
