Timit

 

Lab: Cogan Lab

 

Laptop: Lenovo PC (Zeus or Hades)

 

Program: Psychtoolbox

 

Location: C:/Psychtoolbox_Scripts/timit

 

Filename: playTimitwithQuestions

 

Equipment Used: Photodiode, microphone, 2X amplifiers, speakers

 

Command to run: playTimitwithQuestions(subject, practice, startblock)

     subject = 'D#'

     practice = 0 (no) or 1 (yes) 12 trials

     startblock = 1 for beginning, or n if interrupted and resuming task 

 

Duration: 4 blocks total (81 trials per block); ~45 minutes total if answering immediately (*self-paced)

 

Instructions:

You will listen to short sentences spoken by a variety of different speakers. Randomly, there will be a question based on the previous sentence. Press the left arrow key for YES or the right arrow key for NO. There will be a total of 4 blocks that last about 7 minutes each. You can stop at any time, and we encourage you to take breaks between blocks.
