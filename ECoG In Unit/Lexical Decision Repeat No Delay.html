Lexical Decision Repeat No Delay

 

Lab: Cogan Lab

 

Laptop: Lenovo PC (Zeus or Hades)

 

Program: Psychtoolbox

 

Location: C:/Psychtoolbox_Scripts/Lexical_Repeat/Lexical_DR_NoDelay_Button_2x

 

Filename: Lex_DecisionRepeat_NoDelay_Mouse_2x

 

Equipment Used: Photodiode, microphone, 2X amplifiers, speakers

 

Command to run: Lex_DecisionRepeat_NoDelay_Mouse_2x(subject, practice, startblock)

     subject = 'D#'

     practice = 0 (no) or 1 (yes) 12 trials

     startblock = 1 for beginning, or n if interrupted and resuming task 

 

Duration: 4 blocks total (126 trials per block); ~40 minutes total

 

Instructions:

If you see the cue Yes/No, press the left arrow key for a word (Yes, it is a word) or the right arrow key for a nonword (No, it is not a word). 

 

If you see the cue Repeat, please repeat the word.

 

Respond as quickly as possible.

 

If you see the equals sign (=), just listen and do not respond.
