Sternberg Neighborhood

 

Cogan Lab

 

PC: Lenovo (Zeus or Hades)

 

Program: Psychtoolbox

 

Location: C:/Psychtoolbox_Scripts/Neighborhood_Sternberg

 

Filename: SternbergTask_ver7

 

Equipment Used: photodiode, microphone, 2X amplifiers, speakers

 

Command to run: SternbergTask_ver7 (subject, practice, startblock)

     subject = 'subject ID' (D#)

     practice = 0 (no) or 1(yes) 4 trials

     startblock = 1 for beginning, or n if interrupted and resuming task

 

Duration: 5 blocks total (32 trials per block); ~45 minutes total

 

Instructions:

The word "Listen" will appear on the screen and then disappear. You will hear a series of words, non-words (funny sounding words that do not have an English meaning)in a list sequence. After a short quiet period the word "Probe" will appear on the screen and, you will hear one word or non-word. If the word/non-word played was part of the previous list sequence, press the left arrow key (the YES key). If the word/non-word was not part of the previous list sequence, press the right arrow key  (the NO key). Respond as quickly as possible. There are 5 blocks total that take about 8 minutes each. You can stop at any time, and please use the breaks between blocks if you want to.
