Phoneme Sequencing

 

Lab: Cogan Lab

 

Laptop: Lenovo PC (Zeus or Hades)

 

Program: Psychtoolbox

 

Location: C:/Psychtoolbox_Scripts/phoneme_sequencing

 

Filename: phoneme_sequencing_revised2018

 

Equipment Used: Photodiode, microphone, 2X amplifiers, speakers

 

Command to run: phoneme_sequencing_revised2018(subject, practice)

     subject = 'D#'

     practice = 0 (no) or 1 (yes) 6 trials

     startblock = 1 for beginning, or n if interrupted and resuming task 

 

Duration: 4 blocks total (52 trials per block); ~30 minutes total

 

Instructions:

Please repeat each speech sound after the Speak cue appears. You can stop at any time, and please take breaks between each block. Press any key to start.
