Sentence Rep

 

Lab: Cogan Lab

 

Laptop: Lenovo PC (Zeus or Hades)

 

Program: Psychtoolbox

 

Location: C:/Psychtoolbox_Scripts/SentenceRep

 

Filename: SentenceRep_revised2018

 

Equipment Used: Photodiode, microphone, 2X amplifiers, speakers

 

Command to run: SentenceRep_revised2018(subject, practice)

     subject = 'D#'

     practice = 0 (no) or 1 (yes) 12 trials

     startblock = 1 for beginning, or n if interrupted and resuming task 

 

Duration: 5 blocks total (54 trials per block); ~40 minutes total *The experimenter will need to manually exit the script

 

Instructions:

You will see the word "Listen" appear on the screen. If you see the equals sign (=), do nothing. If you see the word Mime, just move your lips to mimic the word, but do not make any noise. If you see the word Speak, repeat the word or phrase that you heard.
